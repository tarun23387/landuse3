Moradabad Development Authority

Project Detail

  • Objective
  • Methodology

The objective of the project was the mapping of the master plans as received from the concerned authority.1 Colony layout received from Muzzafarnagar Development Authority were also digitized for city planning. All of this delivered to the authority helped in its capacity building and policy making.

  1. Master plan, colony layouts are acquired from the concerning authority.
  2. The maps are georeferenced over the satellite image.
  3. The georeferenced map is digitized i.e. all the features given in the layout are digitally mapped.
  4. In colony layouts the plot numbers are attached to the digitized map.
  5. The master plan made was then classified according to its land use and major boundaries were demarcated.

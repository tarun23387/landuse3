Jhansi Development Authority

Project Detail

  • Objective
  • Methodology

The objective of the task i.e Cadastral mapping was the mapping of villages (khasras) under the development area of the Jhansi Development authority. It aimed to deliver a single seamless mosaic file of all the villages overlaid on the satellite image. It helped in clear demarcation of the plots in the concerning villages and thereby caters to various needs of the development authority and other departments presently.

  • The cadastral/revenue maps are acquired from the concerning authority.
  • Th maps are georeferenced over the satellite image.
  • The georeferenced map is digitized i.e. all the features given in the layout are digitally mapped.
  • Data base attached to the plots is namely: Plot numbers, village code (if any),etc.
  • All the villages are mosaicked and made seamless i.e. boundary of all the villages is matched.
